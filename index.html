How to Play Beyond Bingo

Beyond Bingo is a challenge to complete the bingo card. A random football player will appear at the top and you must match that footballer to 1 of the 16 categories on the bingo card. Further random footballers will appear until you exhaust the full list of football players or complete the bingo card.

For example, if Cristiano Ronaldo randomly appears and there's Ballon d'or or Juventus on the bingo card, you must carefully consider which selection to make as you can only pick 1 per footballer.

Categories may include, teams, countries, trophies and awards.
